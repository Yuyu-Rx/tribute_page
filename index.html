Elizabeth Blackwell timeline

  • 1821 - Born in Bristol, England
  • 1847 - Becomes the first woman to attend medical school in the United States
  • 1849 - Publishes her thesis on typhoid fever in the Buffalo Medical Journal and Monthly Review
  • 1857 - Opens the New York Infirmary for Women with her younger sister Emily
  • 1874 - Works together with other people to create the first medical school for women in England, London School of Medicine for Women, for which she acts as the Chair of Hygiene
  • 1910 - Dies at the age of 89
